Getting Started

Get home in 4 easy steps

1. Find property

Search for properties or new projects by selecting locations to find your preferred home type.

3. Move in

Make a deposit in cash and move in. You can pay the agreed balance as monthly, quarterly or annual rent.

2. Make offer

When you find a suitable property, tell us how you want to pay for it and click on the make offer button. We would appraise your offer and communicate to you our decision within 24 hours.

4. Own it

Possession of homeownership documents at the end of the contract period or when you have completed the agreed payment, whichever is earlier.
